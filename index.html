Project 1

Sustainability Project: Modular Housing Initiative in Lagos, Nigeria
Objective: the project aims to address the severe housing shortage affecting approximately 5 million people living in informal settlements or without proper housing in Lagos, Nigeria.
Solution: to counter this issue, the project proposes the construction and deployment of modular houses. These houses are designed to be affordable, sustainable, and quick to construct, significantly improving the living conditions for Lagos residents.
Impact: the project not only aims to provide more residents with affordable and sustainable housing but also sets a precedent for future urban development initiatives that prioritize environmental sustainability and efficient resource use. The project includes detailed sketches of the modular housing units, illustrating the integration of solar panels and rainwater harvesting systems, highlighting the practical application of sustainable technologies in everyday living spaces.
